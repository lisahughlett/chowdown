title: Peanut Butter Cookies

tags: dessert
recipeYield: 3 dozen

ingredients:
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup peanut butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tsp vanilla
- 1 1/2 cup flour

directions:
- Combine ingredients and bake 375 degrees for 7-9 minutes.
